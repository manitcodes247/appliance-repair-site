Everything you want to know about our home appliance repair service in Mumbai.

How much does appliance repair cost in Mumbai? ▾

No hidden charges, ever. Washing machine repairs typically range ₹500–₹3,000, AC service ₹800–₹4,000, TV repairs ₹700–₹8,000 depending on the issue.
Do you offer same-day appliance repair in Mumbai? ▾
Yes! We offer same-day doorstep repair across all major areas of Mumbai. Book your service before 2:00 PM and our certified technician will visit your home the same day. We cover Andheri, Borivali, Bandra, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Powai, Malad, Kandivali and all surrounding areas.

Absolutely. Every repair by FixIt Mumbai comes with a 90-day service warranty. If the same issue recurs within 90 days of our repair, we will fix it again at no additional charge. We also only use genuine OEM spare parts that carry their own manufacturer warranty.

Booking is simple. You can (1) Call us directly at +91-9820-012345, (2) Fill the online booking form on this page, or (3) WhatsApp us at the same number. Share your appliance details, location, and preferred time — we'll confirm within 15 minutes and dispatch a technician.

Yes. All our technicians are factory-trained and certified for the appliance brands they service. They are also background-verified and carry official FixIt ID cards. You'll receive the technician's name and photo before their arrival for added peace of mind.
